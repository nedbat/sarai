I am a licensed clinical psychologist, trained psychoanalyst, and research psychologist in private practice in New York City.

Treatment

I provide individual psychoanalytic psychotherapy to adults. I specialize in resolving long-term depression, calming anxiety, helping people find meaning in their lives, and living more in line with their values and ideals. I have experience working with patients from all ethnic backgrounds, and with all age groups.

Research

I provide research consulting services in the fields of psychology, psychiatry, education, and nursing, having worked on both doctoral dissertations and funded grants. I can help with developing a research question, organizing literature reviews, creating an experimental design, performing statistical analyses, written and graphical presentation of results, editing final products for APA style, and completing grant applications.
